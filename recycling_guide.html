What can I recycle?

Adbeam rewards students for recycling various materials. Here's what you can recycle on campus to earn points.

Plastic

Most plastic items with recycling symbols 1-7 can be recycled through campus bins.

  • Water and soda bottles
  • Food containers
  • Detergent bottles

Paper & Cardboard

Clean paper and cardboard products are highly recyclable and earn significant rewards.

  • Notebooks and paper
  • Shipping boxes
  • Paper bags

Aluminum & Metal

Metal recycling is especially valuable due to its high reusability and energy savings.

  • Soda and beverage cans
  • Food tins and cans
  • Clean aluminum foil

Glass

Glass containers and bottles can be recycled indefinitely without losing quality.

  • Beverage bottles
  • Food jars
  • Glass containers

Electronics

Special e-waste collection points allow safe recycling of electronic devices.

  • Batteries
  • Phone chargers
  • Small electronics

Special Materials

Some hard-to-recycle items have special collection processes and earn bonus points.

  • Ink cartridges
  • Textbooks
  • Clothing items

Recycling tips for students

Maximize your impact and rewards with these helpful recycling practices.

Quick recycling best practices

  • Rinse food containers before recycling to prevent contamination
  • Remove bottle caps before recycling plastic bottles
  • Flatten cardboard boxes to save space in recycling bins
  • Keep paper dry and clean for effective recycling
  • Check the bottom of plastic items for recycling numbers (1-7)
  • Separate different types of materials (e.g., remove plastic windows from paper envelopes)
  • Use campus e-waste collection points for electronics and batteries
  • Set up a mini-sorting station in your dorm room or apartment

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about campus recycling and the Adbeam app.

Where are recycling bins located on campus?

Recycling bins are located throughout campus, including in all residence halls, academic buildings, the student union, library, and dining areas. The Adbeam app features an interactive map showing all recycling locations.

How do I earn points for recycling?

Simply scan the barcode of recyclable items using the Adbeam app before placing them in the appropriate recycling bin. The app automatically credits your account with points based on the item type and weight.

What if an item doesn't have a barcode?

For items without barcodes, you can use the manual entry option in the app. Simply select the material type and approximate size/weight of the item, and submit a photo for verification.

How long do points take to appear in my account?

Most points appear in your account immediately after scanning. For manually entered items, points are typically credited within 24 hours after verification.

How do I redeem my points for rewards?

Navigate to the Rewards section in the Adbeam app to browse available rewards. Select your desired reward and tap "Redeem." You'll receive a digital voucher or QR code that can be presented at participating campus locations.
